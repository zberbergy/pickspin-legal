Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This privacy policy describes how the PickSpin (“the app”) mobile application collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. About the App

PickSpin is a mobile app that lets you make decisions by spinning a wheel. You create a wheel, add options, and spin to pick one at random. The app does not require an account. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers.

2. Data We Collect

  • Data stored on your device: Your wheel title, options, and preferences (such as sound and haptics on/off) are stored only on your device and are not sent to our servers.
  • Advertising: The app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Depending on the choices you make in the consent messages shown in the app, these ads may be personalized (based on your activity and interests) or non‑personalized. AdMob may collect data such as device identifiers, coarse location, and usage data for ad delivery, frequency capping, measurement, and (where allowed) personalization. We do not use this data on our own servers. AdMob’s processing is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy. You can also learn about how Google uses data in advertising at How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps.
  • In-app purchases: If you make a purchase (e.g. to remove ads), the transaction is processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple’s privacy policy and terms apply to those transactions.

3. How We Use Data

Data stored on your device is used only to run the app. We do not share your data with third parties, except for advertising services such as Google AdMob which may process data as described above. Ad networks such as Google AdMob may process data according to their own policies to deliver, measure, and (where allowed by your consent) personalize ads.

4. Consent and Your Choices

When you first open the app (and from time to time later), you may see a consent message asking whether you agree to personalized ads or prefer non‑personalized ads if applicable. Your choice is stored on your device and shared with Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) and AdMob so that ads are shown according to your preferences and local regulations.

You can change your consent choices later by using the consent options or messages provided by Google in the app (if applicable) or by changing your device‑level privacy settings, including Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission settings.

5. Data Security

Your wheel and option data stays on your device, so its security depends on your device’s security.

6. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this privacy policy. We may notify you of important changes in the app or on the store listing.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the developer or contact information on the app store page.
