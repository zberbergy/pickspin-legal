Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This privacy policy describes how the PickSpin (“the app”) mobile application collects, uses, and protects your data.

1. About the App

PickSpin is a mobile app that lets you make decisions by spinning a wheel. You create a wheel, add options, and spin to pick one at random. The app does not require an account. We do not collect or store your personal data on our servers.

2. Data We Collect

  • Data stored on your device: Your wheel title, options, and preferences (such as sound and haptics on/off) are stored only on your device and are not sent to our servers.
  • Advertising: The app displays non‑personalized ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect limited data such as device identifiers and usage data for ad delivery, frequency capping, and basic measurement. The app does not use this data for personalized advertising or cross‑app tracking. This is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy. You can also learn about how Google uses data in advertising at How Google uses data when you use our partners’ sites or apps.
  • In-app purchases: If you make a purchase (e.g. to remove ads), the transaction is processed by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details. Apple’s privacy policy and terms apply to those transactions.

3. How We Use Data

Data stored on your device is used only to run the app. We do not share it with third parties. Ad networks may process data according to their own policies.

4. Data Security

Your wheel and option data stays on your device, so its security depends on your device’s security.

5. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13.

6. Changes

We may update this privacy policy. We may notify you of important changes in the app or on the store listing.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through the developer or contact information on the app store page.
